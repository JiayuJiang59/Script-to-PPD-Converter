Before you rely on this PPD, please note:
  1. Some content — tables, charts, images, SmartArt — can't be pulled into text. Check each slide against the original PPTX using the Slide # column and fill those in by hand.
  2. When a slide has several text boxes or shapes, the order of text in a cell follows the file's reading order, which isn't always top-to-bottom on the slide. Skim for anything out of sequence.
